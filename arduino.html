What is arduino?

Arduino has over the years released over 100 hardware products: boards, shields, carriers, kits
and other accessories. In this page, you will find an overview of all active Arduino hardware, including the Nano, MKR and Classic families.

What is an Arduino used for? Arduino is an open-source electronics platform based on easy-to-use hardware and software. Arduino boards are able to read inputs - light on a sensor, a finger on a button, or a Twitter message - and turn it into an output - activating a motor, turning on an LED, publishing something online.
